Solar Installation Process Guide

What to Expect When Going Solar

While the process of installing solar panels involves multiple steps, our team handles the details to make your transition to clean energy as smooth as possible. Here's what to expect during your solar journey.

Step 1: Initial Consultation

What Happens:
  • Review your energy needs and goals
  • Analyze utility bills to understand consumption patterns
  • Evaluate your property for solar suitability
  • Discuss available financing options
  • Answer any questions about the solar process

Timeline: 1-2 hours

Your Role: Provide utility bills, share energy goals, ask questions

Step 2: Site Assessment & System Design

What Happens:
  • Detailed roof inspection and measurements
  • Shading analysis using advanced tools
  • Electrical system evaluation
  • Custom system design based on your needs and site conditions
  • Presentation of system layout and production estimates

Timeline: 1-2 weeks

Your Role: Provide access to your property, review and approve the design

Step 3: Permitting & Approvals

What Happens:
  • Preparation of required technical documentation
  • Submission of permit applications to local authorities
  • Utility interconnection application filing
  • HOA approval process (if applicable)
  • Addressing any engineering or compliance requirements

Timeline: 2-8 weeks (varies by jurisdiction)

Your Role: Sign necessary applications, possibly assist with HOA approval if needed

Step 4: Installation

What Happens:
  • Delivery of equipment to your property
  • Installation of mounting system on your roof
  • Placement and securing of solar panels
  • Installation of inverter and electrical components
  • Wiring and electrical connections

Timeline: 1-3 days for most residential installations

Your Role: Provide access to your property and electrical panel

Step 5: Inspection & Approval

What Happens:
  • Final inspection by local building department
  • Utility inspection and approval
  • Installation of utility net meter (if applicable)
  • System testing and quality assurance
  • Permission to Operate (PTO) from utility

Timeline: 1-4 weeks after installation

Your Role: Possibly be present for inspections (not always required)

Step 6: System Activation & Orientation

What Happens:
  • Official system activation
  • Setup and tutorial of monitoring systems
  • Walkthrough of system components and operation
  • Review of maintenance guidelines
  • Completion of paperwork for incentives and warranties

Timeline: 1-2 hours

Your Role: Participate in the orientation, learn system basics, download monitoring app
