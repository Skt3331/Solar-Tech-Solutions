The Smith Family: 90% Reduction in Electric Bills

5.8 kW System $175/month Savings 4.2 Year Payback

The Smith family of four in suburban Phoenix was facing summer electric bills exceeding $300 per month. After installing a 5.8 kW rooftop solar system, their summer bills dropped to less than $30, and they even receive credit from their utility during spring and fall months.

System Details:

  • 18 high-efficiency monocrystalline panels
  • South-facing roof installation
  • Smart monitoring system
  • Battery backup for evening power use

Installed: June 2021

Johnson Residence: Solar + Smart Home Integration

7.2 kW System $210/month Savings 3.8 Year Payback

The Johnsons wanted to integrate solar with their smart home system to maximize energy efficiency. Our solution paired solar production with automated home controls to optimize energy usage based on solar availability.

System Details:

  • 22 bifacial solar panels
  • Integrated battery storage system
  • Smart home integration with automated load shifting
  • EV charging station powered by solar

Installed: March 2022

Martinez Home: Overcoming Limited Roof Space

4.6 kW System $135/month Savings 5.1 Year Payback

The Martinez family had limited suitable roof space but still wanted to maximize solar production. Our team designed a custom system using high-efficiency panels and optimizers to generate maximum power from the available space.

System Details:

  • 14 high-efficiency (22%) solar panels
  • Power optimizers on each panel
  • Custom mounting to maximize usable roof space
  • Energy management system to prioritize essential loads

Installed: October 2022
