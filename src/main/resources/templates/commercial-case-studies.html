Mountain View Retail Complex

250 kW System $4,500/month Savings 5.2 Year ROI

This multi-tenant retail complex was facing rising energy costs that were being passed on to tenants. By installing a large rooftop solar array, the property management company reduced common area charges by 65% and created a more attractive lease proposition for environmentally conscious retailers.

System Details:

  • 625 commercial-grade solar panels
  • Flat roof ballasted mounting system
  • Three-phase string inverters with monitoring
  • Annual production of 375,000 kWh

Installed: April 2021

Precision Manufacturing, Inc.

425 kW System $7,200/month Savings 4.8 Year ROI

This manufacturing facility operates energy-intensive equipment during peak daytime hours. Solar installation allowed them to offset a significant portion of their energy consumption and shield operations from utility rate increases, while fulfilling corporate sustainability commitments.

System Details:

  • 1,150 high-output solar panels
  • Combined rooftop and carport installation
  • Integration with existing energy management system
  • 100 kWh battery storage for demand charge reduction

Installed: August 2022

Greenview Office Park

175 kW System $3,200/month Savings 6.1 Year ROI

This office complex leveraged solar as a key differentiator in a competitive commercial real estate market. The installation not only reduced operating expenses but also helped attract premium tenants with corporate sustainability goals, resulting in higher occupancy rates and lease values.

System Details:

  • Innovative solar canopy design over parking areas
  • EV charging stations powered by solar
  • Smart building integration for optimal energy usage
  • Real-time energy dashboard in building lobby

Installed: January 2023
