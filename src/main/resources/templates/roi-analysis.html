Key Factors in Solar ROI

System Production

The amount of electricity your system generates depends on:

  • System size (kW)
  • Panel efficiency
  • Local sunshine hours
  • Panel orientation and tilt
  • Shading considerations

Electricity Cost

Higher electricity rates mean greater savings:

  • Current utility rates
  • Historical rate increases (national average: 2.5-3.5% annually)
  • Time-of-use considerations
  • Demand charges (commercial)

Incentives

Available incentives dramatically improve ROI:

  • Federal Investment Tax Credit (30%)
  • State and local tax credits
  • Utility rebates
  • Performance-based incentives
  • SRECs (Solar Renewable Energy Credits)

Property Value

Solar increases home resale value:

  • Average premium: 4.1% of home value
  • Studies show $5,000-$20,000 value increase per installed kilowatt
  • Faster home sales for solar properties
  • Tax exempt in many states
