How Solar Energy Works

1. Solar Panel Installation

Solar panels are installed on your roof or ground mount system to capture sunlight throughout the day.

2. Converting Sunlight to Electricity

Photovoltaic cells in the panels convert sunlight into DC (Direct Current) electricity.

3. Inverter Technology

The inverter converts DC electricity into AC (Alternating Current) power that your home can use.

4. Powering Your Home

The converted electricity powers your home appliances and devices. Excess power is sent to the grid or stored in batteries.

Benefits of Solar Energy

Environmental Impact

Reduce your carbon footprint and help protect the environment for future generations.

Financial Savings

Save money on electricity bills and potentially earn from excess power generation.

Energy Independence

Reduce reliance on the grid and protect against rising energy costs.
