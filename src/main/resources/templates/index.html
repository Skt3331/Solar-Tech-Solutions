How Solar Power Works

1. Solar Collection

Solar panels convert sunlight into direct current (DC) electricity through photovoltaic cells.

2. Power Conversion

Inverters transform DC electricity into alternating current (AC) for home use.

3. Energy Storage

Excess energy is stored in batteries for use during nighttime or cloudy days.

4. Power Distribution

Clean solar energy powers your home, with excess fed back to the grid.
