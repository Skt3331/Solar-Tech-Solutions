Solar Warranty Information

Understanding Your Solar Warranty Coverage

Solar energy systems are a significant investment, which is why they come with comprehensive warranty coverage to protect your investment for decades to come.

Types of Solar Warranties

Product Warranty

Covers defects in materials and workmanship of the solar panels themselves.

  • Typically 10-12 years
  • Covers manufacturing defects
  • Includes replacement costs
  • May be extended by manufacturer

Performance Warranty

Guarantees the power output of your solar panels over time.

  • Usually 25 years
  • Guarantees 80-85% production after 25 years
  • Covers gradual efficiency loss
  • Protects your energy production

Inverter Warranty

Covers the device that converts DC power from panels to AC power for your home.

  • String inverters: 10-12 years
  • Microinverters: 25 years
  • Power optimizers: 25 years
  • Extended warranties available

Workmanship Warranty

Covers the quality of installation work performed by your solar installer.

  • Typically 5-10 years
  • Covers installation issues
  • Includes roof penetrations
  • Varies by installation company
